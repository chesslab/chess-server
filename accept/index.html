/accept

Accepts an invitation to play online with an opponent.

params

hash

The unique hash of the game.

username

The username accepting the invitation.

Usage

Example

ws.send('/accept "{\\"hash\\":\\"16539195\\",\\"username\\":\\"normal_magpie\\"}"');
